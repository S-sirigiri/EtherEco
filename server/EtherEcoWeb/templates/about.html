About EtherEco

EtherEco is an open-source chatting platform, built with the goal of connecting individuals across the globe in a secure and friendly environment. It's designed to be user-friendly and free for everyone to use.

You can access our source code and contribute to EtherEco's development on our Github repository. We believe in the power of collaborative development and welcome developers from around the world to help us improve and expand EtherEco.
